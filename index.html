SociVerse AR/VR and AI Metaverse

SociVerse AR/VR and AI Metaverse

SociVerse is a futuristic vision of a decentralized Metaverse created for game enthusiasts to own, play, and earn in any part of the Metaverse Gaming World.

Have you ever desired to own a race track, a sports stadium, or a beach resort? Now your desires can be made virtual reality with SociVerse.

SociVerse is an entertainment ecosystem; a combined mix of Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR) technologies, as well as the possibilities of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and machine learning.

Players will have the opportunity to make their dreams a reality.

Be a part of the SociVerse and embrace your passion, gain experience, and earn; all at once!

Earning opportunities

THE NATIVE TOKEN OF SociWorld

Bloktopians will be able to buy and sell SOCI via both Decentalized and Centralised exchanges. All SOCI from initial real estate purchases will be publicity burnt decreasing the overall supply of the token.

SOCI will also be required to purchase items from within SociWorld for example; upgrading real estate space or purchasing items from within the marketplace. This is what makes SOCI valuable and necessary for the ecosystem to operate.

BUY, SELL & LEASE VIRTUAL REAL ESTATE

SociWorlds will be able to purchase real estate following the SOCI token distribution event (TGE).

The real estate will be a valuable commodity within SociWorld allowing SociWorlds to speculate on the property and either resell for profit or lease for passive income opportunities.

RECEIVE INCOME VIA ADVERTISING

Within SociWorld one of the key earning opportunities to investors is via advertising.

SociWorld will work with key brand partners and give them a unique opportunity to advertise within SociWorld.

A proportion of these advertising fees generated will be returned to SociWorld token holders.

token utility

SOCI is the native token of SociWorld. SOCI will be required to purchase or lease all real estate within SociWorld.

SociWorlds will be able to purchase items from the SociWorld marketplace, via a NFT mechansim.

SOCI is a utility token and offers holders a wide range of features within SociWorld.

SociWorld will work on a Governance model. NFT holders will form part of that Governance council. Each NFT will vote via a DAO.
